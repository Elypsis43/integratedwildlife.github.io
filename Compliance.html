Agency-Facing Documentation

Regulatory Alignment.

Integrated Wildlife Systems (IWS) operates in strict accordance with NYS Department of Environmental Conservation (DEC) and USFWS monitoring standards.

Permitting & Legal Framework

EchoLink deployments are designed to fall under established federal and state research authorizations:

  • USFWS Section 10(a)(1)(A): Compliance for recovery permits involving Myotis sodalis and M. septentrionalis.
  • NYS DEC Part 175: Alignment with Special License requirements for collection or possession for scientific purposes.
  • IACUC Protocols: Hardware design vetted for Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee approval cycles.

Biosecurity & Pd-Control

Preventing the anthropogenic spread of Pseudogymnoascus destructans (Pd) is paramount. Our hardware protocol includes:

  • Sterile Single-Use: Nodes are delivered in vacuum-sealed, irradiated packaging to ensure zero pathogen introduction.
  • Bio-Hybrid Disposal: Chitin/Hemp housing disintegrates in situ, removing the need for post-season cave entry to retrieve hardware.
  • Non-Invasive Attachment: Microneedle "Hilbert-lock" bypasses the need for high-stress collars or invasive ear-tagging.

Spectrum & RF Compliance

Utilizing the Sony ALT1350/1550 SoC to ensure seamless integration with modern communication infrastructure:

  • 3GPP Release 18: Compliance with 5G Non-Terrestrial Network (NTN) standards for direct-to-satellite IoT.
  • FCC Part 15: Low-power "Ghost Protocol" emissions to prevent RF interference with other telemetry or sensitive site equipment.

Data Sovereignty & Biosecurity

Habitat coordinates for federally listed species are critical biosecurity assets. EchoLink data is **AES-256 encrypted** at the node level. Full location telemetry is only accessible via federated dashboards restricted to authorized NYS DEC WNS teams and specific state health officials.
