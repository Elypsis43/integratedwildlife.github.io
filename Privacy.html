Privacy Protocol.

1.0 Data Sovereignty

At Integrated Wildlife Systems (IWS), we believe biological data is a sovereign asset. Our EchoLink platform is built on the principle of "Privacy by Design," ensuring that sensitive environmental intelligence is protected from unauthorized access.

2.0 Collection Scope

IWS nodes collect only high-resolution acoustic and thermal telemetry required for White-Nose Syndrome (WNS) surveillance. We do not collect:

• Human voice data or identifiable human imagery.
• Commercial location data irrelevant to karst biology.

3.0 Security Measures

  • End-to-End Encryption: All biosensor data is encrypted at the source node using AES-256 standards.
  • Habitat Obfuscation: Critical habitat locations (hibernacula) are only accessible to verified agency stakeholders.
  • Non-Persistent Storage: Raw sensor data is purged from intermediary satellite relays immediately upon successful delivery to IWS secure servers.

4.0 Third-Party Sharing

IWS does not sell, trade, or monetize institutional data. We share information only with:

• State/Federal Agencies: Per pilot agreements (e.g., NY-DEC, USFWS).

For questions regarding our data protocols or to request the deletion of your institutional record, please contact our Data Privacy Officer:
