Privacy & Security Policy

Classification: PUBLIC // Effective: January 2026

1.0 Data Philosophy

At Integrated Wildlife Systems (IWS), we recognize that ecological data is a matter of national and environmental security. Our EchoLink platform is built on the principle of Minimum Necessary Collection. We do not track users; we track environmental health indicators.

2.0 Information We Collect

When you interact with the IWS Pilot portal, we collect limited data necessary for institutional collaboration:

  • Institutional Identity: Name and professional email address for agency verification.
  • Network Metadata: IP addresses and access logs (retained for 30 days) to prevent unauthorized access to pilot telemetry.
  • Inquiry Content: Information provided via secure registration forms.

3.0 Telemetry Security

The EchoLink platform transmits data from wild subjects directly to LEO satellites. This data stream is handled with the following security measures:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All biosensor data is encrypted at the source node using AES-256 standards.
  • Habitat Obfuscation: Critical habitat locations (hibernacula) are only accessible to verified agency stakeholders.
  • Non-Persistent Storage: Raw sensor data is purged from intermediary satellite relays immediately upon successful delivery to IWS secure servers.

4.0 Third-Party Sharing

IWS does not sell, trade, or monetize institutional data. We share information only with:

• State/Federal Agencies: Per pilot agreements (e.g., NY-DEC, USFWS).
• Formspree: Our secure gateway for handling initial inquiry transmissions.
